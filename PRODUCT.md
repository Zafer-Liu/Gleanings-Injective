# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Players experience a short, story-led cultural RPG on desktop. They explore a Fujian old-wine memory, collect chapter medals, then optionally connect an EVM wallet to preserve and share selected achievements.

## Product Purpose

Gleanings turns a player's understanding of cultural stories into a personal, portable collection. Story progress remains local and frictionless; only a consciously selected medal is minted as an Injective EVM collectible for sharing and verification.

## Brand Personality

Intimate, handcrafted, quietly futuristic. The product should feel like a treasured family archive with a precise, trustworthy chain record—not a speculative trading interface.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic crypto dashboards, noisy token price displays, forced wallet prompts, and neon-fintech visual language. Never make blockchain a prerequisite for playing the story.

## Design Principles

1. Story first; chain actions are an optional epilogue.
2. Make ownership legible through a clear local-to-chain progression.
3. Treat each medal as a cultural keepsake, not a financial instrument.
4. Give every wallet and transaction state a plain-language explanation.
5. Keep sharing and verification one deliberate action away.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, clear status text, and reduced-motion support. Wallet status must never be indicated by colour alone.
